Norwegian Forest cat

This cat’s ancestors were bred by Vikings 2,000 years ago. Nice and furry, this cat can stand harsh frost and is a great hunter.

Himalayan cat

This breed looks much like a Persian cat, but it’s characterized by blue eyes and colorpoint coloring (light body with dark face, feet, ears, and tail). Himalayan cats were bred in 1950 in the USA. Himalayans are tender, obedient, and friendly cats with a calm temper.

Scottish Fold

The calling card of Scottish Folds is cute ears that, unlike other cats, don’t stick up — they hang. This unusual feature is a result of genetic mutation. These cats are extremely clever. They easily get along with other family members and are always ready to play.
